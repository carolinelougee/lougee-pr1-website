sustainable solutions

We know you have a green thumb, but there is always room to grow! Get even more green by checking out these sustainable gardening tips.

plant organic.

Plant organic plants and seeds, use safe pest control, rotate crops yearly, or draw in some pollinators. Check out this list for more.

Organic Thread

save water.

There are lots of options... drip irrigation systems, watering your pots in the afternoon, recycling rainwater, xeriscaping, mulching, and more.

H2O Savers Thread

plant perrenials.

They grow bigger and better each year, giving you an opportunity to divide the plant, share it, or plant elsewhere in your ever-growing garden.

Perrenials Thread

care by composting.

Compost will add nutriet rich humus to your soil while also reusing what you already have. Learn proper composting techniques below.

Composting Thread

native plant guide.

Native plants thrive in their suitable climates, meaning less work, less water, and a happier ecosystem. Find out which plants are native to your area today!

Native Plant Thread
